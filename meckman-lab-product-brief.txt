MECKMAN LAB - PRODUCT BRIEF
================================

Project Overview
----------------
Meckman Lab is a personal portfolio website showcasing experimental engineering projects that combine hardware, software, and AI technologies. The site serves as a digital laboratory space to highlight innovative work in robotics, automation, and intelligent systems.

Brand Identity
--------------
Name: Meckman Lab
Tagline: "Experimental Engineering Studio"
Domain: Meckman.org
Visual Theme: Clean, minimalist black and white aesthetic

Target Audience
---------------
- Engineering professionals and peers
- Potential collaborators and clients
- Technical recruiters
- Students and researchers in robotics/AI fields
- Anyone interested in practical applications of automation technology

User Experience Goals
---------------------
- Immediate understanding of the lab's focus and expertise
- Easy navigation between different projects
- Professional presentation suitable for portfolio sharing
- Mobile-responsive design for various device viewing
- Fast loading and smooth interactions

Design Principles
-----------------
- Minimalist aesthetic emphasizing content over decoration
- High contrast for readability (dark backgrounds, light text)
- Generous space and clean typography
- Card-based content layout for better organization

Content Strategy
----------------
- Focus on practical, real-world applications
- Emphasize the intersection of hardware and software
- Highlight autonomous systems and AI integration
- Demonstrate engineering problem-solving approach
- Balance technical detail with accessibility

Success Metrics
---------------
- Professional presentation of engineering capabilities
- Clear communication of project scope and technical skills
- Demonstration of both breadth and depth in engineering disciplines

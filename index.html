Hand Tracker

Computer vision and skeletal tracking for hand detection

Hand Tracker uses the forward-facing camera on a laptop and smart phone to demonstrate computer vision and skeletal tracking for hand detection. Real-time analysis captures hand movements and joint positions, showcasing the capabilities of machine learning in web applications.

Finance Teacher

Interactive finance learning with real market data

Finance Teacher is a visual web-based learning app that helps students understand company performance and valuation. Enter any stock ticker to explore four educational charts: price history, fundamentals, P/E ratios, and ROE trends. Built with vanilla HTML/CSS/JS and the Finnhub API for real-time market insights.
